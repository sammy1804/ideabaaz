The Event

India's Most Ambitious Film & Creative Industry Event

A flagship initiative under the Delhi Film Policy, organized by DTTDC in association with the Government of Delhi and managed by Wizcraft Entertainment.

Held at Bharat Mandapam, Pragati Maidan, one of India's largest international convention venues, it brings together filmmakers, producers, investors, storytellers, and creative entrepreneurs from around the world.

"It is where cinema meets commerce and creativity meets capital."

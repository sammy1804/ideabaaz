What is Ideabaaz?

Ideabaaz connects creators, founders, and innovators with global platforms to showcase their ideas and gain industry exposure. We believe in providing a national stage for emerging startups to pitch, innovate, and connect with the titans of industry.

Through our strategic partnerships with ZeeTV and Zee5, we bring the excitement of startup pitching to millions of screens across the nation.
